The problem

Most people find out about their policy gaps during a claim.

Hidden clause

Room rent sub-limits

₹10L health cover but a ₹3,000/day room rent cap. One hospitalisation can leave you paying ₹2–3L out of pocket.

Missed coverage

Employer cover blind spot

Your group health cover ends the day you resign. Most professionals have no backup and a 30-day window with zero cover.

False security

Co-payment clauses

Policies that require you to pay 10–20% of every claim. Buried in 60 pages most people never open.

Critical gap

No term life cover

An endowment or ULIP is not term insurance. If you have dependents and no term cover, your family is exposed.
